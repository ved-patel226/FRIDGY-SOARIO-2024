Elite Tracking

FRIDGY is a web application that helps you manage your fridge. It allows you to keep track of the food you have, the expiration dates, and the recipes you can make with the ingredients you have. It also helps you reduce food waste by reminding you of the expiration dates of your food items.

Identifying Cultured Foods

FRIDGY uses a barcode scanner to scan the food items in your fridge. It then stores the information in a database and displays it on the web application. You can add, edit, and delete food items, as well as search for recipes based on the ingredients you have.
